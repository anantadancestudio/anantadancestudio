About the Director

Meet the heart behind Ananta Dance Studio

Meetal Dave

Creative Director & Lead Instructor

Ananta Dance Studio is led by a passionate and versatile instructor with over 10 years of formal training in Bharatanatyam, culminating in the prestigious Nrityakala Bhushan honor. With a lifelong dedication to dance, her experience extends beyond classical training to include Bollywood, Bhangra, Garba, hip hop, and modern styles.

Meetal's background blends a strong technical foundation with creative expression, shaped by years of choreography for both personal celebrations and professional performances. She has also planned and executed large-scale cultural productions at the collegiate level, demonstrating leadership, organization, and a deep commitment to sharing the richness of dance with diverse audiences.

Through teaching, she aims to create an inclusive and engaging environment where students build confidence, discipline, and a love for movement.
